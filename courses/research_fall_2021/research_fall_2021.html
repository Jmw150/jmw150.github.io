Ece 696 Active Program Synthesis (Masters Research)

This fall the research focus is making formal methods technology more user friendly.

To me, this is about improving the dialectic between computers and people. Logic stuff can have a fairly clean relation to programs. But there is information loss when trying to construct a solid process. Such as: would the user know what they want? And is the burden of specification fairly distributed between person and machine?
