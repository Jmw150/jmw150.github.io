Research
 
Areas of research: Algebraic Topology, Algebraic Geometry, Mathematical Physics, Higher Stuctures.

In particular:

  • Feynman Categories
  • String Topology and Related Operations such as Deligne Conjectures
  • Hopf Algebras
  • Quantum Cohomology and Mirror Symmetry
  • Stringy Phenomena for Stacks/Orbifolds
  • Geometry of Moduli and Teichmueller Spaces
  • Noncommutative Geometry  
  • Nano Wire Networks and Topological Insulators
  • 2+1 dim TQFT and its Extensions

Member of the Purdue Quantum Science and Engineering Institute

Co-Chief Editor for Higher Structures

Area Editor the European Journal of Pure and Applied Mathematics
