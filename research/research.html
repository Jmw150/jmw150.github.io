I am an algebraist. I like abstract algebra and computers.

Research Interests

Most of what I like is in abstract algebra, algebraic geometry, and mathematical logic. I also like automation. Program synthesis (automated synthesis of programs) is my day job. I am pretty curious about how to use automated reasoning systems to help discover new things, use and verify old ideas, and generally make my life easier.
